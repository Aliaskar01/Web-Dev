Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Ground Beef 500g
Tomato Sauce 1 cup
Onion 1 (chopped)
Garlic 2 cloves (minced)

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a large pan, sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Mix the sauce with the spaghetti and serve hot.

Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1.5 cups
Cocoa Powder 3/4 cup
Eggs 2
Milk 1 cup

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs and milk, then mix until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased pan and bake for 35 minutes.
  5. Cool and frost as desired before serving.

Caesar Salad

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Romaine Lettuce 1 head
Croutons 1 cup
Parmesan Cheese 1/4 cup
Caesar Dressing 1/3 cup

Steps:

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce.
  2. Mix the lettuce, croutons, and Parmesan cheese in a large bowl.
  3. Add Caesar dressing and toss until evenly coated.
  4. Serve immediately.

Source: Caesar Salad Recipe
